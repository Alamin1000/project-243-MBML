About

Bumblebee ai is a groundbreaking startup that has developed a bio-mimicking pollination technology. Solving growers’ major challenge of natural pollinators scarcity and honeybees inherent inefficiency, we improve crops productivity & quality, enable control over the entire pollination process and support sustainability goals.

Our client-base comprises some of the world leading Avocado and Blueberries growers, showing up to 30% increase in their yields and an improvement in the number of large size fruits.

How it works

01. Planning ahead

Our agronomical knowhow combined with environmental data provide an output of the exact window of time for pollination, every single day.

02. Crop-driven pollination

Our advanced tools are bio-mimicking the bees work, ensuring every flower is pollinated.

03. Controlling the process

Our tools are equipped with advanced GPS receivers that enable the grower to monitor the pollination work, thereby improving control and yield prediction.

Value Proposition

Increased Productivity

Up to 20% increase in growers productivity, using the same irrigation and fertilization inputs

Improved Quality

A positive shift in the distribution of large and small fruits, resulting in much higher selling prices

Control & Certainty

The data growers are provided with enables them to stay in control over the pollination

Support SDG

Pollination has been identified as directly contributing to food security (SDG2) and biodiversity (SDG15)
